Amritsari Kulcha

This flaky flatbread is topped with a spicy potato filling and is often served with butter or makhani sauce.

Amritsari makhani

This rich and creamy gravy is made with butter cream & spices, and it is often served with paneer or chicken.

Amritsari lassi

This thick yogurt drink is flavored with cardamom it is a refreshing way to cool down on a hot day.

Kheer

This rice pudding is a popular dessert in India, and it is often made with milk, sugar, and spices

Dal makhani

This lentil stew is made with butter, cream, and spices, and it is a hearty and flavorful dish.

Tandoori chicken

It is marinated in yogurt cooked in a tandoor oven. It is a popular dish in Amritsar & throughout India.
